Description

Maze Infinite Puzzle is a cozy, endlessly replayable maze-puzzle you can slip into any time.
Each run generates a fresh labyrinth and follows a gentle, flowing difficulty curve no spikes, no stress. Prefer to start fresh? Reset the flow at any moment and begin again from a smaller maze.

There are no timers and no combat just smooth movement, soft neon, and a 10-track relaxing soundtrack to keep you focused while you unwind and collect Steam Achievements along the way.

How it plays

  • Find the portal. Read the maze, plan a route, and move with simple keyboard controls.

  • Endless procedural mazes. New layouts every time, scaling up gradually for a natural sense of progress.

  • Reset anytime. Instantly restart the size/complexity flow whenever you want a clean slate.

  • Two clean viewpoints. Toggle between a clear Top-Down angle and a stylish 2.5D tilt.

  • Helpful, optional hints. Tap a subtle breadcrumb trail when you want a nudge then turn it off and keep exploring.

  • Relaxing, readable visuals. Minimalist forms and calm neon color palettes that shift from level to level.
