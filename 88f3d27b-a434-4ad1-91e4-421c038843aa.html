Description

Create and Cultivate Your Dream Farm!

Get your hands dirty with our revolutionary soil and compost system. Cultivate over 60 exclusive crops with the perfect soil mix, grow more than 50 types of fruit trees, and nurture 15 different berry bushes. Your farm is your creative masterpiece!

Join Forces with Your Pet!

Your pets are more than just cute companions—they're your trusted allies! Ride your quirky chicken in thrilling races, get your sturdy horse to help chop trees, and battle legendary bosses alongside your powerful cow. Together, there's nothing you can't conquer!

Build a Community Full of Heart!

Forge lasting friendships with over 50 quirky townsfolk, and maybe even find love among the 34+ marriage candidates. Create your own story and start the next chapter of your journey in this welcoming community.

Discover a World of Unique Animals!

Welcome to a world where no two animals are alike! Each creature boasts its own vibrant colors, patterns, and features. Breed them to create new combinations, and who knows? You might even discover a delightful mutation! Where did that second head come from?

Cook Up Culinary Wonders!

Head to your cozy kitchen and discover over 200 unique recipes from around the world. From simple snacks to gourmet meals, there's always something new to create. Ready for a delightful dish?

Master Fun Mini-Games!

Take a break from farming and enjoy over 8 exciting mini-games in the Game Hall. Tap, whack, and toss your way to fabulous rewards and endless fun!

Embrace Epic Adventures!

Equip yourself with weapons from 11+ different classes and explore 5 procedurally-generated environments, each home to a legendary boss waiting to challenge you!

Express Your Style!

Personalize your look with over 90 stylish hats! From the Clucky Cap to the Road Cone, each hat not only looks great but also provides stat boosts. Choose your favorite and head out in style!

Farm Smarter, Not Harder!

Enjoy features designed to enhance your farming experience:

  • Overflow backpack storage
  • Evolving tools
  • Game speed slider
  • Adjustable field of view, zoom, and tilt

Engage in Exciting Animal Auctions!

Join lively auctions to buy and sell a variety of items and animals. Make strategic decisions to grow your farm's prosperity and enjoy the thrill of the auction!

Celebrate Your Achievements!

For all the completionists out there, unlock 97 achievements, 5 exclusive Steam Boss Cards, and Cornucopia Steam Badges. Show off your skills and complete your collection!

Immerse Yourself in an Ever-Growing World!

With regular updates, there's always something new to discover. New content, features, and improvements keep your farming journey fresh and exciting.

Start Your Farming Journey Today!

Don’t wait—Cornucopia awaits! Build your dream farm, make lasting friendships, and discover endless adventures. Dive in now and see where your journey takes you!
