Description

Margo is back!
Armed with a strong hunger for vindication as well as a dubious psychotherapeutical coping mechanism, she sets out to tie up the loose ends she left behind.

While getting used to her new life as a capable, collected young woman, an unexpected letter draws her into a mysterious plot. Facing many new demanding challenges, will she stay on top of things?

Equip Margo with all the best gear (the Scarf of Social Bravery, anyone?), find just the right artifacts, solve the occasional puzzle and settle the score with her old nemeses.

Midnight Margo is a narrative-driven roleplaying adventure with a touch of absurdity. It deals with topics such as existential anxiety and social conflict, but also offers occasionally dark humour as well as puzzle-solving.

Midnight Margo features

  • A superb cast of voice actors that bring the dialogues to life
  • Unique photo collage graphics, mixed with handpainted elements
  • An atmospheric soundtrack by composer Jacob Lincke
  • Moral dilemmas
  • A plethora of colourful characters
    and more.
