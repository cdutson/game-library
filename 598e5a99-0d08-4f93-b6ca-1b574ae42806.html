Description

Wanderful is about forging your very own adventure by placing tiles in procedurally generated worlds, exploring what encounter might be waiting around the next corner. You decide the pace: Marvel at the beautiful landscape, be amazed by the obscure things that you might encounter on your journey or simply race to the end of the world. Are you ready for a challenge whilst besting the ultimate adventurer’s high score? How far can you venture before running out of tiles?

Your adventure starts out in the middle of a huge map only equipped with a few tiles that are able to lift the shrouds around it. Tiles are placed to uncover landscapes, creatures and wonders to generate more tiles allowing you to continue your journey.



Eventually you will encounter different biomes and will learn about the limitations, the day and night cycle and the hidden powers of each tile.



In Wanderful you create your own story. Do you build a peaceful village that houses a gigantic chicken or do you dare to enter the spicy dunes and cultivate a graveyard for fallen dreams? It is in your hand.

Features

  • Create an ever sprawling world full of small adventures and big surprises
  • Laid back and soothing gameplay
  • Session lengths between 5-30 minutes
  • Play highly tactical or just build your ideal little world
  • Try to beat your adventure high score with each new run
  • Plenty of unlockable biomes, tiles and wonders
  • Interactive soundtrack
  • Flexible Camera system that lets you enjoy the view you prefer
