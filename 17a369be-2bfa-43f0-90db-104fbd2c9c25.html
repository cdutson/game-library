Description



Dive into an emotionally charged story set in the serene backdrop of Moonglow Bay, in this relaxing, slice-of-life fishing RPG!

Take on the role of a rookie angler, driven by the final wish of your beloved partner, as you navigate through the challenges of a town on the brink of bankruptcy. Equipped with only your journal and trusty rod, embark on a journey to uncover the secrets hidden beneath the waves of Moonglow Bay.

Embark on an Aquatic Adventure


  • Discover the Depths: Cast nets, set traps, line, and ice fish to discover over 151 aquatic species lurking beneath the surface.
  • Preserve and Present: Cook your catch of the day or proudly show them off at the local aquarium.
  • Explore Enigmatic Locales: Traverse the Glacier and Sulphur zones, unravel the mysteries of the Astral Clouds, and prepare for encounters with the Mythical Monsters of Moonglow.
  • Share the Adventure: Team up with friends in local co-op and embark on a heartwarming journey together.

    Revitalise the Bay


    • Celebrate the Centennial: Help revitalise the once-thriving fishing town, breathing new life into its weathered buildings.
    • Meet the Townsfolk: Befriend the locals and accept quests through the bulletin board to restore the Bay’s fractured community.
    • Equip for Success: Stock up on bait, tackle, and ship upgrades to prepare for the challenges ahead.

      Discover the Heart of Moonglow


      • Vivid Voxel Art: Immerse yourself in Moonglow Bay's stunning visual style complimenting your adventure every step of the way.
      • Sumptuous Soundtrack: Lose yourself in Lena Raine's enchanting score that seamlessly flows with the rhythm of your journey.

        Explore the Picture Perfect Update


        • Photomode Delight: Capture the beauty of Moonglow Bay from every angle with our new photomode feature.
        • Customise Your Haven: Personalise your storefront with new decorations that reflect your unique style and personality.
        • Unearth Ancient Mysteries: Join Marina on a thrilling quest to uncover the skeleton of a long-lost sea creature. Piece together its bones, decode its legend, and embark on a journey that spans land and sea in search of elusive fossils.
