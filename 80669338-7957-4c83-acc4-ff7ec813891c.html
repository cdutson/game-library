Description

The Adventures of Bluke Bifton is a hand-drawn, episodic adventure game set in a world of absolute absurdity. Journey across the once-peaceful Isle of Flungo in Chapter 1, crafting alliances (or making enemies) with a wide cast of strange, fully-voiced characters. With a major focus on interactions and choices, every decision you make impacts your journey. It's time to meet some new friends, make some unexpected choices, and stand up against some violent, quite-possibly-insane foes!

Explore the Isle of Flungo


The Isle of Flungo is open to explore, whether it be on foot, or by (extremely run-down) vehicle. As Bluke, you'll navigate varying locations in a side-scrolling, point-n-click perspective, able to click on any object or character of interest to engage with it. Then, hop into the Munchmobile to take a road trip throughout the overworld - featuring optional objectives, a shop, and plenty of weird interactions!

Make unpredictable choices and be met with equally-unexpected consequences


Want to take on the role of a pacifist? Achieve your goals through violence? Maybe you just want to keep everyone alive... with a emphasis on choice and a branching storyline, it's up to you to decide how to navigate Bluke's chaotic journey.

Engage in genre-shifting minigames and hone your skills


Saving the Isle isn’t all work! Genre-shifting minigames are sprinkled throughout Bluke’s journey to spice things up. Try for a high-score in Quinston's Arcade, and compete with your friends on the leaderboards. Rhythm game showdown, anyone?
