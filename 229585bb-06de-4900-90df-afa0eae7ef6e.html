Description

🛂 CHECK DOCUMENTS, BUILD A STATION 🛠️

Welcome to a cozy, pixel-art universe where you're the guardian of a bustling space station! Your main job is to carefully check documents for every alien who wants to dock. You'll encounter 15 unique races, each with their own rules. The gameplay blends cute, casual inspection puzzles with light management.

A successful check earns you resources to build and expand your station block by block. Furnish it with capsule hotels, karaoke bars, and stylish decor to keep your visitors happy!

🤔 CHOOSE YOUR FOCUS

Love the thrill of inspection? Handle every passport yourself for maximum profit.

Prefer being an architect? Hire staff to automate check-ins. You can then focus on building the perfect station, managing production chains, and handling the daily chaos of a galactic hub.

💥 DEAL WITH UNEXPECTED EVENTS

Life in space is unpredictable. Be ready for anything, from a beetle parasite outbreak to mysterious traders and runaway inflation. Watch out for criminals like pyromaniacs and thieves trying to slip past your security!

🎮 PLAY YOUR WAY

Follow a story campaign with multiple endings. Or create your own adventures in the endless sandbox mode. The choice is yours. How will you run the galaxy's most interesting transit point?
