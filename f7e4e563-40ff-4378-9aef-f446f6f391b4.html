Description

AFTERBLAST is a challenging FPS rogue-lite action game. Play solo or in co-op. Features dynamic gameplay with randomly generated dungeons, so don't hesitate to obtain the power, unlock new loot, and find a way to save your planet. Stand up against the gods and fight against hordes of monsters.

Game Features

  • Intense, fast-paced FPS Roguelite
  • Co-op: gather your team to beat all the upcoming challenges
  • Roguelite Mechanics: encounter hordes of enemies on randomly generated maps, and discover variety of weapons, items and skills
  • Difficulty Levels: experience varying levels of challenge with four difficulty settings: normal, hard, expert, and nightmare. Each level introduces new enemies with increased damage and health values. Higher difficulty levels reveal new rooms, items, weapons, and abilities
  • Permanent Progression: strengthen up your abilities to conquer the forthcoming adversaries
  • Over 100 Items, both passive and active, to collect and unlock during the game
  • Variety Of Weapons, each having its own unique abilities
  • Enemies: face off against basic foes, elites, mini-bosses and more
  • Enormous Bosses: confront them during epic battles, featuring unique gameplay mechanics
  • Merchant: utilize the services of a friendly merchant to purchase upgrades during your run
  • Special Rooms: fight on challenging arenas, overcome tricky arcade rooms, discover treasure chambers, and more...

Story

Far away, in a vast galaxy full of magic, sits a small and peaceful planet, seemingly abandoned by its Creator... Once full of beauty and harmony, it is now mired in the war and destruction caused by a massive blast...

An evil alliance of gods has set its sights on this world's most precious treasure - the Crystal of Eternity. Using its power, they seek to further their dominance, regardless of the destruction they bring.

Amid the chaos, the chosen ones arise. Guided by an unseen god, they begin a dangerous journey to defend their homeland. Many uncertainties lie ahead, but one truth remains clear - the fate of this planet rests on the shoulders of these heroes. Blessed with the power of immortality, they grow stronger with each rebirth... As they explore the planets of this galaxy in search of more stolen crystals, they encounter allies and enemies alike. The ultimate battle against the invading gods has begun! This is the last glimmer of hope to save the world from catastrophe.
