INAYAH - Life after Gods: A Journey of Survival and Discovery

Dive into an exciting 2D action-platformer with metroidvania elements, where every corner of the post-apocalyptic world comes to life with meticulously hand-drawn visuals. This world is full of dangers, yet its beauty will make you stop and take a breath more than once.

Play as Inayah, an orphaned outcast searching for her lost tribe in a world where brutal clans rule over the ruins of a once high-tech civilization. Along the way, she will face intense trials, deadly battles, and life-changing decisions. Your decisions will shape the outcome of her journey – how will it end?

Explore a vast 2D world with diverse biomes, each harboring secrets. Use Inayah's movement abilities and her drone companion, Sputnik, to uncover hidden paths and treasures.

Inayah’s adaptive Gauntlet can transform into Blades, Flail, or Fists, each offering unique combat and movement abilities. Switch freely between them to craft your own playstyle!

Test your reflexes and strategic thinking against more than 20 unique bosses!  Learn their mechanics, exploit their vulnerabilities, and evolve your strategy through every phase of the fight.

Upgrade your weapon proficiency through a skill tree and equip various enhancements to gain an edge in battle. Experiment with different builds and tailor Inayah’s abilities to match your preferred way of playing!


The game boasts stunning 2D art and meticulously hand-drawn frame-by-frame animations.
This commitment to high-quality aesthetics ensures that each moment in INAYAH is a feast for the eyes, setting it apart from other 2D platformers.


Your choices matter. Interact with the inhabitants of this unforgiving world, uncover hidden lore, and shape Inayah’s destiny. Offer help or follow your own path, and carefully consider the consequences of your actions. Unlock multiple endings and see what fate awaits Inayah and her world.

Embark on an unforgettable journey in "INAYAH – Life After Gods", where challenging trials and emotional stories await on your path to finding home.
