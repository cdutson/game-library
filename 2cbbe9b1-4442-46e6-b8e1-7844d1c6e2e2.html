Description

'Void Diver: Escape from the Abyss' is a multiplayer action game that combines beautiful 2.5D pixel art with the high-stakes tension of an extraction RPG.

The city you once knew has transformed into a massive dungeon swirling with otherworldly forces. As the manager, you must command 'Divers' as they plunge into this perilous space to recover lost artifacts.

Explore, fight, and escape places teeming with aberrations and madness. All for the sake of completing your requests and returning alive.

COMBAT: Action that Tests Your Limits

  • Together or Alone: Cooperate with up to 3 players to conquer dungeons, or choose to explore the depths as a lone wolf.

  • Diverse Styles: Choose from a variety of characters, each with unique skills and narratives, to experience different combat styles.

  • Hardboiled Action: Experience sophisticated, high-impact real-time action set amidst the ruins of the city.

EXPLORATION: Secrets of Distorted Space

  • Otherworldly City: Explore bizarre spatial structures where familiar cityscapes have fused with another dimension.

  • Artifact Recovery: Your primary goal is to find hidden valuables—super-scientific devices, forbidden magic relics, and more—and escape safely.

SURVIVAL: Madness and Curses

  • Diverse Threats: Fight against otherworldly monsters, rampaging machines, urban legends, and humans driven to madness.

  • Mental Pressure: Dungeons consume not just the body, but the mind. Manage the stress that leads to strange phenomena when it hits its limit.

  • The Paradox Curse: Survival includes finding cures for 'Paradoxes'—dreadful curses that linger even after an expedition.

GROWTH: Build Your Own Legend

  • Character Development: Combine exclusive skill trees and traits to maximize your character's potential.

  • Gear Customization: Create a unique build using countless weapons, accessories, and relics obtained from the dungeons.

MANAGEMENT: Antique Store ‘BALUSHA’

  • Shop Management: Run the antique shop ‘BALUSHA’ in the heart of a city where anomalies have become daily life.

  • The Merchant: Support Divers and grow your business by trading artifacts. As you deal in forbidden relics, the city's dark secrets will slowly be revealed.
