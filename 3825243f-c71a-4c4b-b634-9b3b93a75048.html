Description

"Escape Memoirs: Safe House” is a co-operative reverse escape-room puzzle game, where you are a member of a criminal organization who is tasked with fetching some important equipment from one of their safe houses. Unfortunately, the person with the keys to the safe house is nowhere to be seen, so your only option is to break into the safe house to get the tools.

You must make good use of your wits and puzzle solving skills if you are to overcome the challenges that await you.

Solve Puzzles

From deductive reasoning to safes of all kind, you will face a wide variety of puzzles that will test your logical thinking skills, as you try to finish your job.

Explore and Discover

You will get to explore a cozy safe house as you take on its many challenges. There will be plenty of hidden collectibles for you to find as you make your way through the safe house.

Work Together

Play solo or work together with friends, in up to 5-player multiplayer. So, pick up a friend or four, and go burgling together!
