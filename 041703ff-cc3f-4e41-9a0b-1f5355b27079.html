Description

ASTRO ACE is a retro-style addictive arcade space shooter.
From the distant universe, an evil alien fleet is invading the solar system.
You will have to ride a powerful spaceship and fight against endless waves of enemies.





• You have to battle an endless wave of enemies in a universe surrounded by radiation walls. You can use the boost, brake, and left and right turns to avoid attacking enemies or aim behind them.


• There are more than 10 types of weapons: cannon, laser, plasma, and missile, etc. Eliminate enemies with your own way. Don't forget to replenish your ammo and energy to destroy more enemy ships and survive longer.


• When you shoot down 5 Elite ships of the enemy, you can get powerful Wingman support for 15 seconds.


• Make your ship more powerful through various power-ups and prepare for extreme boss battles.


• Level up to use the weapon you want from the start or unlock new ships and equipment for your support.

• Get a higher score than the other aces and rank at the top of the leaderboard.




• Endless waves of a variety of enemies.

• Challenging boss stage battles.

• 10 Playable spaceships.

• 14 unique primary weapons.

• 7 powerful secondary weapons.

• 3 levels power-ups of range, damage, etc.

• Unlockable ships, paint jobs, and support equipment.

• Steam leaderboards, Steam cloud support, and Achievements.

• Keyboard, Gamepad Support.
