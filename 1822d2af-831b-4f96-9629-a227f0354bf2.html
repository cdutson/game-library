Description

Seafrog is a 2.5D action-platformer mashed with the movement of lite skateboarding. Grind, kick-flip and find the flow with a rocket-powered wrench that moves smoother than a buttered beluga!

String together combos by landing grinds on fat rats or rusty pipes to catch big air and fuel up your rocket-wrench! Use the fuel to boost through enemies, fly straight up walls, and stick to ceilings in order to complete over 100 handcrafted challenges!

Master the movement and new abilities to get to tough-to-reach fish filled with upgrades for your wrench and coveted golden ambergris -- also know as a hunk of love straight from the sea! HAR!

Battle a magical Skraken, Metal Beak the Momma Chacken, and... Gerald. Face these colorful 'n crusty foes to retrieve the Master Gears and repair your cannon. Blast yourself to a stranded seaplane to escape the seahole, a mysterious sinking hole in the ocean… before ye end up in Davy Jones' Locker!
