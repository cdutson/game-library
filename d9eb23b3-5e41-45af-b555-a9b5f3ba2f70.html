Description

Step into a world where combat never has to go the same way twice. Engage the lupine enemy in dynamic combat, with responsive and precise battle controls.

Find a host of ferocious enemies, ranging from the smallest minions to hulking monstrosities – with an army augmented by the despicable Doctor Wolf.

Unlock new weapons, skills and abilities to pick up on each run, but Protectors beware! One wrong move and while Omega will persist through death, any modifications collected will be lost as you hit a new Loop with a fresh loadout – it’s time to steel your woollen form, and protect the flock.

After defeating enemies, players will download and evolve critical skills to help you fight through the Loop. Rather than just acquiring new skills, Ovis Loop gives you the choice between passive and active upgrades for each skill.

Decide to buff the skill you have, or add an additional chained skill to carry on carving your way through Doctor Wolf’s minions. Our skill and combo system allows you to play your way. Focus on strengthening just one skill, or embrace the dynamic force of the chain, it’s up to you.

Each Alpha enemy and Doctor Wolf’s augmented monstrosities provide increasingly difficult challenges as you progress through the Loop. As well as harbouring secrets about Ovis Loop’s world and inhabitants, they also learn from your own playstyle.

Whether you like to charge in, use area of effect or dive in and out, each boss will adapt to meet your challenge. Focus on dodging and find a boss that aggressively pursues. If you prefer to rush in, it’ll retreat to find space.

You define the battle, but each Loop is designed to test you with more than simple pattern recognition. Overcome each challenge through intuitive action and a build that works for you.


An unconventional story, maybe, but this is not the tale you have heard before. As well as powerful new skills and abilities, you’ll uncover secrets about Ovis Loop’s post-apocalyptic world and (more importantly) about our hero Omega. Become the Shepherd, protect your flock, and delve into the mysteries you find in each Loop.

Ovis Loop is the chronicle of just one lone sheep, trapped in a brutal, looping war with a lupine enemy. In this fight, you must stand alone, but Ovis Loop gives you the tools to succeed, for those with wool made of steel.
