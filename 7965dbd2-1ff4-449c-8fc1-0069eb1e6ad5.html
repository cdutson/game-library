Description

Ten Bells is a Spot-the-Difference Anomaly Horror

  • Explore the eerie timeless pub

  • If you don't find any anomalies, progress onwards

  • If you spot an anomaly; go back the way you came

  • Evade traps and the inhabitants of the pub

  • Unravel the mystery through collected items 

  • Reach and eventually escape the Ten Bells

...if you can.

Over 55 Anomalies: Discover anomalies to progress and uncover the story. Anomalies can be subtle, surreal, scary or deadly.

Unfolding Narrative: Discover objects that contain story elements, helping you piece together the spooky tale of the pub’s ghostly inhabitants.

Play Time: Perfect for a captivating stream or a chilling solo session, with gameplay ranging from 20 minutes to an hour. Full game from 1 hour 20 minutes to 3 hours.

Will you escape the pub and uncover the dark secrets of Ten Bells? Scour the pub, find the anomalies, and experience the horror for yourself.
