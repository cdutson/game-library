Description

Ghostly Acres is an incremental hotel management game that lets you play at your own pace. Keep a bunch of guests to maximize income or just a few to plan out your next build.

Mel inherited the manor from her Uncle a few months ago. Unfortunately, he still owes a lot of coins to the bank. Oh, and it's haunted by Flip the not-so-spooky Ghost! Determined to keep their home, Mel and Flip decided to team up. With your help, they can pay off the bank for good.

Build beds plus food, drink, and entertainment machines to keep visitors arriving and spending. 

Collect coins, gems, and ectoplasm from machines and beds.

Upgrade Mel, Flip, and other abilities to run more efficiently.

Grow the inn by cleaning off dirty tiles to open up more building space.
