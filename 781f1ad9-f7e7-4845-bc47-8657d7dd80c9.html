Overview

Reborn: An Idle Roguelike RPG blends the long-term satisfaction of idle games with the challenge of roguelike progression and the charm of classic RPG storytelling.

You play as Katie, a Reborn—an immortal warrior sent to protect a quaint, slowly unraveling village nestled on the edge of a dangerous cave. While you're off battling waves of increasingly powerful Creatures, the village moves forward: relationships evolve, secrets bubble to the surface, and a darker truth about your origins begins to emerge.

💤 Idle, But With Purpose

Set your character to auto-fight through an ever-deepening cave and check in when you're ready. Level up your stats, craft and enchant your gear, brew potions, and plan your next run.

⚔️ Die. Rebuild. Go Deeper.

Each run ends in death, but to a Reborn, death is growth. Keep your experience, and customize stronger builds with every rebirth. Tweak your stats, push one floor further, and uncover a little more each time.

🏡 A Village Worth Protecting

Meet 19 unique villagers with evolving relationships and personal storylines. Help them grow, uncover the truth about your shared pasts, and change their futures.

📜 Systems With Depth

  • Six core stats to build and balance.

  • Crafting & gear upgrades with randomized bonuses and enchantable crystals.

  • Potions brewed with a mix of ingredients for temporary, stackable boosts.

  • Questlines that unlock more of the world and the people within it.

🌱 Built For The Long Haul

This is a game designed to be played in short bursts across weeks or even months. No daily login requirements. No monetization. Just a chill, thoughtful loop you can come back to whenever you want.
