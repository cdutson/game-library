About the game

Cute Farmer Life is a cute pixel art farm simulator designed to be played at your own pace. Grow, craft, harvest and talk to the locals while you solve the mystery that hangs over the small town.

Designed to play without worries, in Cute Farmer Life you play as Matt, a cat who doesn't want to go back to the city and moves to a small house in the countryside to live on what he grows. Craft tools, grow rare plants, take care of the animals and discover all that this farm simulator offers you, where crops take seconds instead of days and there are always things to do.
