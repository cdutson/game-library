Description

SKIGILL: a bullet-hell roguelike survivor, with a huge skill tree!

A group of adventurers is trapped in an otherworldly pocket dimension. As they face waves of enemies, their only hope is: The SKIGILL!

The SKIGILL

A vast magic sigil is etched on the battleground. Each one of its magic nodes provides a boon to its user: statistics increase, weapons, and powerful perks.

Defeat enemies, collect resources, and invest them in the nearby nodes.

But be careful where you go next, because you cannot conquer the entire SKIGILL at once. Knowledge of its layout will be crucial to your efficiency in battle and survival!

The Heroes

An unknown number of adventurers are trapped here. Your role is to help them conquer the SKIGILL and survive 15 minutes of unrelenting enemy waves!

Each starts with their own weapon, perk, and position on the Skigill. Thus, each of them have unique gameplay and strategies available, but it is possible to hybridize them by reaching the other character's zone on the SKIGILL.

For now, 3 are playable:

  • The Strongman punches his way through the waves with devastating attacks that turn the enemies into explosive projectiles!

  • The Sorcerer casts lightning, jumping between the enemies and inflicting considerable damage against packs!

  • The Fox is swift and nimble, it catches resources with its hookshot that pierces enemies back and forth. The resources also have an additional healing effect!

The 3 other characters will soon be added.

The Weapons

More than 40 weapons await you in chests scattered all around. Adapt your strategy to the weapons that fate chooses for you. Or reinforce the weapons you already own in an attempt to get your dream build!

The Perks

Reinforce your strategy with more than 30 powerful perks to include in your build! Launch lightning bolts when you collect resources, heal yourself when you open a chest, or make an explosion when you unlock a node!

The meta progression

A second sigil is here to empower your characters further and builds! 

It contains permanent statistics increases, new and specific unlockable weapons, and more characters to play!

As you progress in the meta-sigil and learn new strategies, you can try harder difficulties, which in turn reward you with more resources to build new strategies with!

There is no tutorial, and no text; it will be up to you to experiment and discover synergies!

Music and SFX by Noé Guiton.
